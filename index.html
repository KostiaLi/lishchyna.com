About

A bit about me.

I'm Kostiantyn Lishchyna — a colorist with 15+ years in film, advertising and music video. I started out editing music videos back in 2011, which drew me into cinematography and editing, and eventually into working exclusively with color.

Over the years I've graded for Ukrainian and international artists, directors and brands — music videos, commercials and fashion films, several of them internationally awarded.

I built and calibrated my own grading suite from the ground up, so I understand the craft from the panel to the final master. I work comfortably with any format, camera or footage quality — including graphics, film and 360° video.

My main tool is DaVinci Resolve. What drives me is simple: building images that stay with the viewer.
