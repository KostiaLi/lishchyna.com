About

Hey — great to see you.

My name is Kostiantyn Lishchyna, and I'm a colorist with over 10 years of experience in the film, advertising and music video industries. My journey started in a video rental store during my school years, moved through editing music videos, and eventually led me to cinematography, editing and — exclusively — working with color.

I've collaborated with Ukrainian and international artists and directors, helping them bring their creative visions to life through color. My work spans music videos, advertising campaigns and fashion films for well-known brands, some of which have received international awards.

I built my studio from scratch — selecting and calibrating monitors and essential equipment — which gave me a deep understanding of the technical nuances behind a high-quality final product. I'm comfortable with footage of any format, length or quality, from every type of camera, including graphics, film and 360° video.

My primary tool is DaVinci Resolve, with experience in Adobe Premiere and Final Cut Pro. My passion for cinema and deep experience in color grading let me create unforgettable images that enrich the visual experience of viewers.
